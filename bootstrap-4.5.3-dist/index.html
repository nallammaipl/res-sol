Welcome to DineDivine

Nestled in the heart of Greenway, DineDivine is a sanctuary for food lovers. Our culinary philosophy is simple: create exquisite dishes that ignite your senses and warm your heart.

Our Story

In 2015, passionate food enthusiasts Meena and Raj Kumar embarked on a journey to create a dining experience like no other. With a vision to blend traditional recipes with contemporary flair, DineDivine was born. What started as a small family-owned bistro has now blossomed into a beloved restaurant known for its inviting atmosphere and delectable cuisine.

Our Philosophy

At DineDivine, we believe that great food starts with the finest ingredients. We source locally whenever possible, supporting our community’s farmers and artisans. Each dish is crafted with care, infused with love, and presented with elegance. Our commitment to quality and sustainability is reflected in every bite.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Anil Kumar leads our culinary team with over 25 years of experience. A maestro in the kitchen, Chef Anil combines traditional techniques with innovative twists to create dishes that are both familiar and extraordinary. His dedication to culinary excellence and passion for flavors are the heart and soul of DineDivine.

The DineDivine Experience

Step into our elegantly designed dining room, where warm lighting and modern décor set the stage for a memorable meal. Whether you’re enjoying a casual lunch, a family dinner, or a romantic evening, DineDivine offers the perfect ambiance for every occasion.

Customer Experience

At DineDivine, your satisfaction is our priority. Our attentive staff is committed to providing exceptional service, ensuring that every visit feels special. We strive to make each guest feel like part of our family, with personalized touches that go beyond the plate.
